portrait photography

Portrait is a word of French origin that means a picture or art. It is used to express the depiction of people, or rather the depiction of people's faces. Its goal is to show facial features and expressions. The most important elements that affect the portrait photography is the lighting, as the expression of the face and the personality that it reflects may change with the change of the light falling on it.
